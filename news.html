Plans

I think it's important that I plan often, given that my future appears to change ten thousand times in a single breath.

Career

I am trying to become a research and development engineer for video games. This is how I see my life playing out in broad strokes at the moment.

  • One more year of classes at EPFL.
  • Original research on character animation.
  • An internship at a research institute such as La Forge.
  • Game engine developer based in Switzerland.
  • Return to academia for PhD studies.
  • Teaching position at a meaningful level.

As you go further down the list it's less of a garantee.

Dreams

There are the elements that would infuse my life. These are my polar stars.

  • The study of the material, that from which I am wrought.
  • The study of my self, where awareness begins and ends.
  • The study of the human story, the one that continues in me.
  • The practice of the sword, where my values are tested.
  • The creation of culture, through building and engineering.

If I hold these compasses close and let them guide me I'm certain I will be burn brightly.
