I am a Greek-Australian graduate student at EPFL in Switzerland studying Data Science and Mathematics with a focus on the theoretical side of Machine Learning and Algebra.

I enjoy sharing my insights with others and communicating complex ideas in an accessible manner. Perhaps you have come across my video presentation on Chaotic Attractors, which has garnered considerable attention.

I have a passion for exploring the intersection of data science and mathematics, and I've had the opportunity to study topics such as Machine Learning, Markov Chains, Optimisation, Learning Theory, Number Theory in Cryptography, and Integer Optimization.

I love learning world languages. I'm fluent in English and French, and have intermediate proficiency in Greek, German, and Japanese. Currently, I'm dedicating most of my time to learning Mandarin Chinese, and I enjoy using language as a means to connect with people from different cultures and backgrounds.
