Orfeas Liossatos

I'm a Greek-Australian graduate student at EPFL in Switzerland. I study Data Science and Mathematics, leaning on the theoretical side of Machine Learning, and Algebraic Geometry.

I'm always learning new skills, and I'm always talking about what I learn. I'm best known for my video presentation of Chaotic Attractors.

Interests

Optimization in machine learning is fascinating to me. I enjoy writing proofs in integer programming, running evolutionary algorithms, analysing multi-armed bandit scenarios, and deriving statistical bounds. Simultaneously, I am drawn to the language and ideas of Abstract Algebra.

My hobby is learning and teaching world languages. I speak French fluently; I have achieved an intermediate level of Greek, German, and Japanese; I am currently spending most of my energy learning Mandarin Chinese.
